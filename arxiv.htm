How do I post my manuscript on arXiv?
(Read this article completely then upload your article)

Link to register - http://arxiv.org/edit-user/

Only the original authors are permitted to reproduce the accepted paper with arXiv

  • Accurate meta-data must be included while reproducing the accepted paper with arXiv
  • It is mandatory to acknowledge that the paper is published with International Journal of Engineering Trends and Applications.

IJET License Detials:    This work is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported License.

While uploading the paper on arXiv, the author will be asked to submit meta-data details regarding the paper.

This section will explain the various parameters that the authors will encounter while uploading the paper.
1. Title :   The complete name of the article. Do not use all upper case letters.
2. Authors:    Include the names of all authors. The list of authors must be similar to the one published with IJET. The rule is that all names should be given in the order, FirstnameLastname or FirstnameMiddlenameLastname (where Lastname is your family name).
3. Abstract:    Do not include the word "Abstract". Do not start lines with whitespace (spaces, tabs, etc.) unless you are trying to prevent arXiv's automatic line wrapping. Abstracts longer than about 20 lines will be truncated; abridge your abstract if necessary
4. Comments:   Indicate number of pages and number of figures. Also mention the statement "Published with International Journal of Engineering and Techniques (IJET)"
5. Report-no:    Leave it blank.
6. Category:   Each arXiv article has a primary category and may also have one or more cross-lists to other categories.
7. Journal-ref:  This field is mandatory. Copy the citation data ( popup window's APA Data) available in the citation section on the abstract page (go to respective Volume & Issue in the IJET Archives page)of the article in the IJET website and paste the same.
8. An example of journal-ref is:
P.Mallikarjuna,Dr. B.Uma Maheswar Gowd. "Performance Analysis Of Domestic Refrigerator By Using 662 S- Silicone Grease Coating On The Evaporator". International Journal of Engineering and Techniques (IJET) V1(1): Page(16-22) Jan - Feb 2015. ISSN: 2395-1303 www.ijetjournal.org.Published by International Research Group.
9. DOI:    Leave the field blank
10. ACM-class:    Leave the field blank
11. MSC-class:    Leave the field blank
The authors have to include the following header (format provided) in the paper before reproducing IJET copyrighted articles in arXiv:










 ISO 3297:2007

ISSN: 2395 - 1303

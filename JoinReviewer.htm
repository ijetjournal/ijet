Join as a Reviewer:

Board Member & Reviewer advised to follow the following Instructions:

The editorial Board Member is a key part of IJET journal. Researchers and academicians are invited to participate in the review process of ijet papers. Board members are generally expected to complete their review within a one week period. Please return the article within one week or find alternative reviewer, if you are unable to review. Reviews should be constructive and positive feedback to the authors. The completed review paper should be electronically returned to ijet Managing Editor.

IJET Board Member benefits reduced publication charge. If you would like to become an IJET reviewer, you should send the following information to the ijet editor (editorijetjournal@gmail.com). You will receive an email shortly which lets you know whether you have been selected as IJET reviewer or not.

Board Members & Reviewers duties:

  1. Contribute the popularity of IJET,
  2. Initiate the scholars to put papers in IJET,
  3. Propose some special issues
  4. Board members should help the quality improvement of journal,
  5. Board member should bring IJET as one of leading journal in the world.
